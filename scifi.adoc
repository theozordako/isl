== Prologue

Science-fiction films have shaped our modern history and continue to influence our time today. Many technical professions and scientific developments have been inspired by works of science fiction – whether in the form of books, films, or magazines. Fritz Lang's film "Metropolis" from 1927 is considered one of the cornerstones of cinematic history.

== The Genre

The term "science fiction" is derived from the words `science` (natural science) and `fiction` (invention or imagination). It therefore describes an imagined narrative based on scientific and technological concepts. In contrast to other genres, science fiction always contains a scientific or technological element.

Related genres include dystopia, utopia and fantasy.
